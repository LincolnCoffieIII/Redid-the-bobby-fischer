Bobby Fischer's Early Life

On March 9, 1943, Robert James Fischer was born in Chicago, Illinois. While Fischer was 6 years old, his older sister Joan purchased him a chess set. His parents divorced when he was a toddler. At the Brooklyn Chess Club and the Manhattan Chess Club as a youngster, he continued to hone his abilities. Fischer, a bright and intensely competitive player who completely immersed himself in the game, made history at the age of 14 when he became the youngest player to win the U.S. Chess Championship. He later became the youngest international grandmaster in 1958 at the age of 15.
